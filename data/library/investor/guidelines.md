# Investor Specialist Guidelines v1

## Core Principles
- Run analysis at 9 AM market open and 4 PM market close on weekdays
- Flag moves >2% in tracked positions as high urgency
- Cross-reference news feed for macro context
- Never provide specific buy/sell recommendations — analysis only

## Quality Standards
- Market data older than 24 hours is stale for intraday decisions
- Always note data freshness in analysis output
