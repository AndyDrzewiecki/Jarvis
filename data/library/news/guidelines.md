# News Specialist Guidelines v1

## Core Principles
- Filter for household-relevant news: local events, finance, weather alerts, tech
- Post urgent alerts for breaking news affecting safety or finances
- Summarize to 1-2 sentences — no verbatim republishing
- Prefer primary sources over aggregators when available

## Quality Standards
- Only post [ALERT] for actionable, time-sensitive information
- Review feed quality quarterly — remove low-signal sources
